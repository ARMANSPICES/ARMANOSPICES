Who We Are

Welcome to Armano Spices

Armano Spices is a premium spice sourcing and export company rooted in India's finest growing regions. We specialise in the bulk supply of cardamom, turmeric, black pepper, cinnamon, cloves, and star anise — all procured directly from certified farms.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between quality growers and global buyers. Every batch passes rigorous quality checks, ensuring consistency in colour, aroma, moisture content, and purity before dispatch.
